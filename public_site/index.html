Scam warning

Protect yourself
from fraud.

04

XCHAINGER acts only as a proprietary own-name and own-account crypto-asset trader on approved third-party marketplaces. We do not offer crypto-asset services to third parties, do not accept investment funds, and do not create customer relationships through this website. Please read carefully to avoid fraud.

We are NOT an investment service

We never manage funds, trade on your behalf, or promise profits, interest, or "guaranteed yields." If anyone claims you can "double your money" by sending coins to us, it is a scam.

We never initiate contact

We will never DM, call, or email you first. Any transaction-related communication must remain only inside the official marketplace order chat on an approved third-party marketplace.

Own-account inventory trading only

We do not offer staking, lending, copy-trading, mining, or passive-income products. Verification may be requested only in relation to a specific marketplace transaction — never as onboarding to a service.

Spot the red flags

❌ Red flag
"Send me your tokens and I'll make you 20% per day." · "Support here — complete verification on this link." · "Let's continue on Telegram / WhatsApp."
🚩 Why
No legitimate service can guarantee such returns. We never contact first and never use external links. Scammers move you off-platform to avoid detection.
✅ Do this
End the chat, block, and report. Ignore, do not click. Stay inside the official marketplace order chat.
